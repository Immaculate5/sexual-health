Our Services

  • STI Testing and Treatment

    • Confidential testing for sexually transmitted infections (STIs) including HIV/AIDS, syphilis, gonorrhea, and chlamydia.
    • Medical professionals provide counseling and treatment options based on test results.

  • Contraceptive Counseling and Access

    • Personalized counseling on contraceptive methods.
    • Access to a range of contraceptive options including pills, condoms, injectables, and implants.
    • Information on effectiveness, side effects, and usage instructions provided.

  • Family Planning Services

    • Comprehensive family planning services for informed reproductive health decisions.
    • Counseling, contraceptive options, and referrals for long-term family planning methods.

  • Sexual Health Education

    • Sessions and workshops on safe sex practices, STI prevention, healthy relationships, and consent.
    • Empowerment with knowledge to make informed choices about sexual health.

  • Preventive Health Screenings

    • Regular screenings for early detection and prevention of STIs, cervical cancer, and other reproductive health issues.
    • Conducted by trained healthcare professionals in a confidential and supportive environment.
